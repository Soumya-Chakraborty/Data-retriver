  1. Select the company name below and press Ctrl+Shift+1 (or Cmd+Shift+1 on Mac)

  3. Select the LinkedIn URL below and press Ctrl+Shift+3 (or Cmd+Shift+3 on Mac)

  5. Repeat with other company sections
